# Direct Outreach

Ask for feedback, not promotion. Keep each message short and technical.

## Target Mix

- 10 React/Next.js dev-tool maintainers.
- 10 AI agent/browser builders.
- 5 SEO or AI-search practitioners.
- 5 agency or frontend leads.

## Message Template

Subject: Feedback on AgentNav, agent-readiness for React/Next.js

Hi {name},

I am building AgentNav, an open source React/Next.js library that generates `/llms.txt`, `agent.json`, `actions.json`, JSON-LD, `data-agent-*` attributes, form metadata, safety rules, and an agent-readiness score.

The framing is “Lighthouse for AI agents.”

I would value your technical feedback on whether the metadata model and safety rules make sense for real websites:

https://github.com/mido18/agentnav

One specific question: what metadata should an AI agent need before clicking a button or submitting a form?

Thanks,
Mohamed

## Follow-Up Template

Thanks for taking a look. I am trying to keep the MVP practical for React/Next developers rather than overclaiming what agents consume today.

If any part of the README was confusing, I would rather fix that before the wider launch.
